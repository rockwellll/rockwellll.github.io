Chapter I: Amnesia

The king is dead. With his death the kingdom of Media is undone. Media, one of the kingdoms of the east, located north to the arabian sea, cornered by volatile kingdoms, kingdoms that wanted a share of its wealth, for the land was full of precious things and brought merry to the inhabitants, but it did not fall, with the might of its people they thrived in the face of uncertainties and constant danger on the horizon.

We all knew this day would come. The king--our king has been sick for a long time, getting worse each day. The king who united our people, who made the Media, protected it, has gone, with him the hopes for building a stronger kingdom dies. Our kingdom will be undone, his death will be a forever dark spot in our heart. Spies all around will get this news to other kingdoms, the kingdoms we fought and held for long. They seldom tried to attack us during the lifetime of our king. With his death, they will try to take what belongs to us.

Nay, the cannot, we must not let them, all the work by the king undone by these wretched vagabonds, conquerors. We are our protectors, our saviours, our defenders.
